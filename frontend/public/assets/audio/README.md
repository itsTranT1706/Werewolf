# Audio Assets

Place your audio files here:

## Required Files:
- `intro.mp3` - Dark ritual intro music (plays once, ~30-60 seconds)
- `ambient-loop.mp3` - Ambient background loop (seamless loop)

## Recommended Audio Characteristics:

### intro.mp3
- Duration: 30-60 seconds
- Style: Dark orchestral, ritual drums, ominous choir
- Mood: Ancient curse awakening, foreboding
- Format: MP3, 128-192kbps

### ambient-loop.mp3  
- Duration: 60-120 seconds (seamless loop)
- Style: Dark ambient, subtle drones, distant whispers
- Mood: Lingering curse, eternal darkness
- Format: MP3, 128-192kbps
- Important: Must loop seamlessly without audible gap
